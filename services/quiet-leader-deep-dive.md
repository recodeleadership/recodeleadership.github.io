### Who this is for

This session may be helpful if you:

- Have reflected on your leadership but feel stuck applying it in practice
- Designed an experiment but didn’t quite follow through
- Want to explore what might be holding you back
- Are looking for a thoughtful space to translate insight into action

You don’t need to have attended [Quiet Leaders Lab](/resources/quiet-leaders-lab/).
Anyone who wants to **examine their leadership more closely and take a meaningful next step** is welcome.

### What we’ll do together

During this 50-minute session we’ll:

**Look at what you’ve been noticing**  
What reflections or insights have been emerging for you?

**Explore where things got stuck**  
What made the experiment feel heavy or difficult to follow through?

**Identify what might be underneath**  
Sometimes the barrier isn’t capability — it’s uncertainty, expectations, or something unseen.

**Design a concrete experiment**  
We’ll shape a small, realistic step you can take over the coming month.

The goal isn’t to force change.
It’s to create **a shift that feels possible and true to you**.

### FAQ

**I’m not in a formal leadership role yet—can this still help?**

 Yes. Leadership shows up in many ways, not only in titles. If you’ve ever led a project, guided others, or carried responsibility, this space will be valuable for you.

**I don’t think of myself as “quiet.” Is this for me?**

 If anything here resonates, you’re welcome. Many people find parts of themselves reflected in this description, even if they wouldn’t label themselves “quiet.”

**Will this solve all my challenges right away?**

 No single session can do that. Growth takes practice and time. What this session offers is a next-step action plan that you can practice in the coming months to see what feels right and more authentic to you.

**Why does this cost money?**

 I’ve set the price as accessibly as I can. Coaching is also an exchange of energy—your investment helps you commit to the process, and it supports me to continue offering this work.

**What if I truly can’t afford this?**

 If you deeply resonate but can’t make the investment right now, I keep a few sliding-scale spots available. Please reach out in [this sliding-scale request form](https://forms.gle/ozwneGi6j5bDcjnj7) if this speaks to you.

**Can’t I just design and do the experiment myself without the coaching session?**

 You could. That is exactly what [Quiet Leaders Lab](/resources/quiet-leaders-lab/) help you do as well. But many people do some reflections, experience a new awareness, and then lack the momentum to take action. The value of this session is in integration—guidance to create an action plan to move forward. That’s where the shift happens.

**What if I don’t have an experiment defined yet?**

 That’s okay. We can identify one together in our session. We can explore together what might be weighing on you and where change may be helpful.

**Why are there no available time slots right now?**

I don’t offer refunds, but I completely understand that life happens. If something comes up, I’m happy to help you reschedule your session so we can meet at a better time for you.
